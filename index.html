Hi! I am a first-year Master's student at University of Toyama, advised by Prof. Chao Zhang. I received my Bachelor's degree from Shenzhen Technology University in 2025.

My research interests lie in unlocking the unknown potential of models for various computer vision tasks, including image generation and data distillation. I am also very interested in the intersection of AI+health/robotics, specifically in areas like medical image analysis and robot learning.

Hi! I am a first-year Master's student at the University of Toyama in the KiZulab, advised by Prof. Chao Zhang. I received my Bachelor's degree from Shenzhen Technology University in 2025.
My research interests lie in exploring the latent capabilities of pretrained models for various computer vision tasks, including image generation and dataset distillation. I am also deeply interested in the intersection of AI for Healthcare (e.g., medical image analysis) and Robotics (e.g., robot learning).
